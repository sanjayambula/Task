What is Skildex?

Skildex is a campus-based skill exchange platform where students connect to learn and teach each other. Whether you're great at coding, music, designing, or soft skills – there's someone out there who wants to learn from you, and someone who can teach you something new.

Features

  • Search and connect with skilled peers on your campus
  • Offer and enroll in one-on-one or group sessions
  • Integrated scheduling with Zoom or MS Teams
  • Rate and review each other to build trust
  • Personal dashboard to track learning and teaching
